Paneer Butter Masala

A rich & creamy curry made with paneer, Indian spices, and butter.

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a pan, heat oil and 1 tablespoon of butter over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Stir in the pureed tomatoes, red chili powder, cumin powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  5. Add the cubed paneer and gently mix to coat with the sauce.
  6. Pour in the heavy cream and garam masala. Stir well and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Finish by adding the remaining butter for extra richness.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with naan or rice.
