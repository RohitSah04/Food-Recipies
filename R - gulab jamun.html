Gulab Jamun

A delicious Indian dessert made from milk solids and soaked in syrup.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/4 cup milk (as needed)
  • Oil, for frying
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1 teaspoon rose water
  • Chopped nuts (pistachios or almonds), for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix together the milk powder, all-purpose flour, and baking soda.
  2. Add the ghee and mix until crumbly. Gradually add milk to form a soft dough.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls, ensuring there are no cracks.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan over low heat. Fry the balls until golden brown, turning gently.
  5. In a separate pot, combine sugar and water to make the syrup. Add cardamom powder and rose water, then simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Once the balls are fried, soak them in the warm syrup for at least 30 minutes.
  7. Garnish with chopped nuts if desired and serve warm or at room temperature.
